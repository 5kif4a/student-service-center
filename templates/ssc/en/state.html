Public services

Public services are provided in accordance with the current Rules and standards of public services.

  • Accepting documents and enrollment to higher education institutions for training in educational programs of higher education

  • Accepting documents and enrollment to higher education institutions for training in educational programs of postgraduate education

  • Accepting documents for participation in the competition for studying abroad, including that within the framework of academic mobility

  • Issuing duplicate documents of higher and postgraduate education

  • Providing a hostel for students at institutions of higher or postgraduate education

  • Providing free meals to certain categories of citizens, as well as to persons under guardianship and patronage, students and pupils of organizations of technical and vocational, post-secondary and higher education
