Self-service room

The presence of a self-service room in the SSC permits students, the teaching staff and University employees to receive the necessary certificates such as: the presence or absence of real estate, no criminal record, from anti-tuberculosis, neuropsychiatric organizations, vaccination passport and others.

The SSC employees provide on a constant basis practical and informational- advisory assistance to students and employees when submitting applications, receiving EDS, certificates, and register them in the mobile citizens database through the website https://egov.kz.
